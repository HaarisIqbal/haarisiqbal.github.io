Projects

RetroKings Chess Video Game

Created a 2D Chess video game as part of Royal Hackaway v4, HackathonUK. The game was designed to be an enchanced version of traditional chess, introducing power-ups that can be accessed by a point system. This fundamentally changes the strategy in which the game is played. Some of the power-ups in the game include:

  • Invincibility - For one turn, any color can use their accumilated points to make any piece of theirs invincible.
  • Double Turn - Any color can use their accumilated points to play two moves in their turn (one use only).

Designed and written in C# using the Unity Engine, within 30 hour time constraint. Custom pixel art sprites drawn with Piskel, custom chiptune sounds produced with Bfxr. Worked in a team of two against teams of five, won the position of runner-ups.

You can download and play the video game for windows here.
